Types of Event Insurance Coverage

At SD Group, we offer a range of event insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Event Cancellation Insurance: Covers financial losses due to event cancellations or postponements.
  • Public Liability Insurance: Protects against legal liabilities for injury or damage to third parties during the event.
  • Equipment Insurance: Covers damage or loss of event equipment.
  • Weather Insurance: Provides coverage for weather-related event disruptions.
